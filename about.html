The United Kingdom, commonly known as the UK, is a sovereign country located off the north-western coast of the European mainland. It is a constitutional monarchy, consisting of four countries: England, Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland. It is a member of the European Union (EU) and NATO, and is also a major economic and cultural power.


The UK is a major economic powerhouse and has one of the world’s largest economies. It has a highly developed welfare state and the world’s fifth-largest economy. The UK is also a major player in the world of international politics, and it is a permanent member of the United Nations Security Council.


United Kingdom supports international efforts to promote human and economic development, reduce poverty, and boost shared prosperity around the world.
